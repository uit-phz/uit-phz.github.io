# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What are your business hours?**
A: We're available 24/7 through this chat! For phone support, our hours are 9 AM to 6 PM (Myanmar Time), Monday to Saturday.

**Q: Where are you located?**
A: We are based in Yangon, Myanmar. We ship nationwide.

**Q: Do you have a physical store?**
A: Currently we operate online only, but we're planning to open a showroom soon!

## Orders & Shipping

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Within Yangon: 2-3 business days. Other cities: 5-7 business days.

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes! Once your order is shipped, we'll send you a tracking number via this chat.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing them. After that, please contact our support team.

**Q: Do you deliver internationally?**
A: Currently we only deliver within Myanmar. International shipping is coming soon!

## Payment

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept KBZ Pay, Wave Pay, CB Pay, and Bank Transfer (KBZ Bank).

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, all our payment methods are secure and verified.

**Q: Can I pay on delivery (COD)?**
A: COD is available within Yangon for orders under 50,000 MMK.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the item is in original condition. Contact us to arrange a return.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item.
